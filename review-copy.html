The Veil: Covenant

She was never meant to exist. Now she's the key to stopping an interdimensional war.

Onyx spent her life running from a past she cannot remember and nightmares she cannot escape. When a weekend birthday trip in the mountains ends with her falling off a cliff, she should die. Instead, she finds herself lifted in the arms of a stranger with wings.

The Veil series follows Onyx as she embarks on an epic urban fantasy adventure alongside Raphael, a Celestial warrior, and the mysterious Chthon named Lucian. A war is reignited in Los Angeles between winged guardians, horrifying creatures, and the Chthon who were trapped beyond the Veil.
