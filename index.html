The Veil: Covenant

She was never meant to exist. Now she's the key to stopping an interdimensional war.

Onyx spent her life running from a past she cannot remember and nightmares she cannot escape. When a weekend birthday trip in the mountains ends with her falling off a cliff, she should die. Instead, she finds herself lifted in the arms of a stranger with wings.

The Veil series follows Onyx as she embarks on an epic urban fantasy adventure alongside Raphael, a Celestial warrior, and the mysterious Chthon named Lucian. A war is reignited in Los Angeles between winged guardians, horrifying creatures, and the Chthon who were trapped beyond the Veil. As realms of light and shadow collide, every book peels back deeper conspiracies and forces brutal choices about loyalty, power, and what salvation really costs.

Meet The Characters

Onyx

A guarded Los Angeles journalist haunted by her mother's death and prophetic nightmares, Onyx is caught in the middle of a war that has stretched for millennia.

Raphael

A duty-bound Celestial warrior fascinated by humans, Raphael begins to question his beliefs about the Chthon as he feels a growing connection with Onyx.

Lucian

A dangerously charming Chthon torn between loyalty to his vengeful clan and a hunger for peace, Lucian's bond with Onyx blurs every line between enemy and ally as the Veil begins to crack.

About The Author

Meredith Taylor is the author of The Veil and the Ridgemont University series. She enjoys a good book and characters who are pushed to the limits of love and war. She spends most of her days drifting in her own imagination.

When not writing, Meredith enjoys exotic teas and traveling.
